Real Madrid vs Alavés 2026 live stream: Time, TV channels and how to watch La Liga online

by Lucas Navarrete
Apr 21, 2026, 11:18 AM UTC

Real Madrid host Alavés in what will be the first game after being eliminated from Champions League contention. The race for LaLiga is all but decided but Alavés are still fighting to avoid relegation, so it will be interesting to see what version of Real Madrid shows up tonight.

PREDICTED LINEUPS

Real Madrid predicted XI: Lunin, Carvajal, Militao, Huijsen, Mendy, Tchouameni, Valverde, Güler, Bellingham, Vinicius, Mbappé.

Alavés predicted XI: Sivera, Pérez, Otto, Tenaglia, Parada, Yusi, Blanco, Guridi, Ibañez, Martinez, Boyé.

HOW TO WATCH, STREAM LA LIGA

Date: 04/21/2026

Time: 21:30 CET, 03:30pm EST.

Venue: Santiago Bernabeu, Madrid, Spain.

Available TV: DAZN La Liga (Spain), ESPN Deportes (USA).

Available Streaming: ESPN+ (USA)
