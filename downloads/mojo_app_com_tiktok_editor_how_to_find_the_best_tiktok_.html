How to search and find the best TikTok?

TikTok, one of the most popular social networks, offers many videos and high-quality content. For entertainment or inspiration, are you looking for the best TikTok videos? Here are some tips to find the most popular content on TikTok. You will also find in this article the most popular TikTok hashtags and challenges, and the most popular influential “TikTokers” on the platform.

How to find the best TikTok videos?

TikTok has a Discover section. Tap the icon with a magnifying glass in the menu at the bottom of the screen, right next to the home icon. The app lists several of the most popular hashtags. Thanks to that feature, you can easily discover trendy videos and content on TikTok. Click on hashtags (preceded by #) to discover related content. You can even add hashtags to your favorites to find them easily later.

It is also possible to directly search for your keywords. Simply tap the search bar at the top, enter a keyword that interests you, and validate the query. You can display several lists related to the search: a list of accounts containing the keyword, a list of sounds, a list of hashtags, or even videos. Moreover, the platform created its  TikTok editor, allowing you to create your own and unique video, at any time.

To find the best TikTok videos, learn more about challenges and hashtags

To maintain one’s audience’s attention, TikTok offers challenges every week. They are inspired by the phenomenon of the meme and offer the community to make video sketches on a given theme, such as the famous Ice Bucket Challenge which had mobilized the Facebook community for many weeks.

The concept of TikTok challenges is part of the app identity. In addition to its viral aspect, which is very popular, the success of the challenges lies in the fact that the platform offers gifts to the directors of the most watched and shared videos. Challenges also help you find out the most popular TikTok photo editing trend.

How to discover a new TikTok challenge?

The challenges can be found on the app's home page or in the search bar which, like most social networks, includes the latest trends, trending hashtags, and popular TikTok videos. TikTok's official account @OfficialChallenge also posts new challenges every week that are almost instantly relayed by the community.

What are the most famous TikTok challenges?

Some TikTok challenges have been very popular among the community. While many challenges simply consist of dancing to the rhythm of specific music, such as #BadBoyChallenge or #DuraChallenge, others are more original. 

  • Shoe Challenge: Users had to make a short video where they changed shoes every second.
  • Hide And Seek Challenge: you had to disappear and then reappear unexpectedly.
  • Eat On The Beat Challenge: This TikTok challenge asked users to eat to the beat of a song.

TikTok also offers challenges related to current events, such as the #DontJudgeMeChallenge which invites you to reveal an unflattering self-image and then put yourself in your best light. This challenge aimed to prove that appearances are often misleading. Finally, the application also surfs on popular events such as the #HalloweenBattle, a challenge in which TikTok indicated that “Halloween is the time of year when real beings reveal themselves” and invited its members to post videos of their events, costumes, and make-up. Finally, some challenges are really out of the ordinary, such as the #WhistleChallenge which asked Internet users to perform choreography with their ten fingers.

Find the most popular TikTok content creators

Initially simple and anonymous for the most part, these social media management experts have become real phenomena on social networks thanks to their millions of followers. Mega communities offer enormous possibilities in terms of influencer marketing. Here are the top 10 of the biggest TikTok influencers in the world. They know how to make a good TikTok for sure!

1. Charli D'amelio (@charlidamelio, 97.0 million followers)

The first most followed person on TikTok, Charlie D’Amelio is only 16 years old but already has 97 million followers! It all started in June 2019 when she posted videos of her choreography on viral songs on the platform. Immediate success! In less than a year, the young American rose to the top of the social network ranking. Since then, her notoriety on TikTok and her dancing skills have allowed her to appear with many celebrities. You can see her in several clips of Jennifer Lopez and Bebe Rexha. Use the TikTok edit app to create high-quality content!

2. Addison Rae (@addisonre 67.4 million followers)

Second, in the global ranking of mega influencers, Addison Rae started making videos on TikTok in July 2019. Since then, her number of followers has continued to climb. Dance, song, the 20-year-old “TikTok famous” knows how to do it all. Her well-stocked address book should keep her on the top of the social network for a long time to come.

3. Zach King (@zachking, 52.1 million followers)

A filmmaker and illusionist, Zach King first showed off his talents on the Vine platform. Today he is showing off his videos full of special effects (thanks to the TikTok video editor) to over 52 million TikTok followers, fascinated and fond of his visual magic tricks. A recipe that works since Zach King occupies the top 3 of the most followed tiktokers in the world.

4. Loren Gray (@lorengray, 49.2 million followers)

As you probably know, the old TikTok was called Musical.ly. It was on this platform that Loren Gray started to share her singing skills. At only 18 years old, she became a real star of social networks and knew how to keep and entertain her followers. She counts on TikTok almost 50 million people and tons of views on each video.

5. TikTok (@tiktok 48.5 million followers)

Small curiosity about the ranking, TikTok itself is in the top 10 of the most followed accounts. The official account of the application quite simply posts news for its users.
