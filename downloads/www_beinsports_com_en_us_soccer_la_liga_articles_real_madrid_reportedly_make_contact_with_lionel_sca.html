Real Madrid Reportedly Make Contact with Lionel Scaloni

04/27/2026 - 05:18 PM
- by beIN SPORTS USA

  • Real Madrid begin planning for the future, with Lionel Scaloni emerging as a top candidate to replace Álvaro Arbeloa.

Real Madrid have started making moves ahead of next season, and one of the names gaining traction is Lionel Scaloni, the current head coach of Argentina. According to multiple reports, the Spanish giants have already made initial contact with the World Cup-winning manager as they consider a potential change on the bench.


The team’s current situation — out of the LaLiga title race and with little left to fight for — has raised serious doubts about the future of Álvaro Arbeloa, who took charge during a difficult period.

The profile Real Madrid are targeting

The Real Madrid board, led by Florentino Pérez, is reportedly searching for a high-profile manager capable of leading a new competitive project. Names such as Jürgen Klopp, Didier Deschamps, and Cesc Fàbregas have all been mentioned, but Lionel Scaloni stands out due to his recent success on the international stage.



The Argentine coach has earned global recognition after winning the Copa América and the FIFA World Cup with Argentina, managing elite talents like Lionel Messi and proving his ability to handle star-studded squads.

A complicated move

Despite the interest, securing Scaloni would not be straightforward. The coach remains under contract with the Argentine Football Association through the 2026 World Cup, making any immediate move difficult.

Scaloni himself has stayed focused on his current role, avoiding speculation about his future and emphasizing his commitment to Argentina.

Arbeloa under pressure

The links to Lionel Scaloni also highlight the uncertainty surrounding Álvaro Arbeloa. The Spanish coach has struggled to close the gap with FC Barcelona, who currently dominate the league.

With five matches remaining and no realistic title hopes, Real Madrid have already begun evaluating their season and considering major changes.
