    FAQ

    What is SongFromTikTok.com?

    SongFromTikTok.com is an online TikTok song finder designed to help you identify the song from a TikTok video using only a link. By pasting a public TikTok URL, our system analyzes the audio and determines which music from TikTok is playing. The result includes the song title, artist name, and links to listen on popular streaming platforms.

    How do I find the song from a TikTok?

    To find the song in a TikTok, copy the TikTok video URL from the TikTok app or your browser and paste it into SongFromTikTok.com. After clicking "Find song", our TikTok music finder analyzes the audio of the video and automatically identifies the song playing in the TikTok.

    Does this work with "original sound" TikToks?

    Yes. Many TikTok videos are labeled as original sound even though they use real, released music. Our TikTok song finder does not rely on TikTok's sound labels or descriptions. Instead, it analyzes the actual audio from the video, which allows us to often identify the song from TikTok even when the platform does not show the song name.

    Does this work with every TikTok video?

    SongFromTikTok.com works with most public TikTok videos that contain music. However, our TikTok song finder may not work with private or deleted TikToks, region-restricted videos, videos without music or with only spoken audio, or songs that are not available in our music recognition databases.

    Do I need to install an app?

    No. SongFromTikTok.com is a fully web-based TikTok music finder. You do not need to download or install any app. The entire TikTok song finding process works directly in your browser on desktop or mobile.

    Is there a usage limit?

    Normal usage is completely fine. To maintain fast processing and reliable TikTok song recognition for everyone, extremely heavy or automated usage may be limited.
