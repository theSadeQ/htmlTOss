⚽ Meet the biggest Soccer Influencers on Tik Tok

 

Raquel Benetti (Brazil)
Previous brand campaigns: VISA, Nike, Visit Qatar, FIFA

Raquel Benetti is one of the world’s most recognized female freestylers and an amazing talent from Sao Paulo, Brazil. Her videos have been seen by millions around the globe and she’s worked with huge brands across all 5 continents.

Daniel Got Hits (New York - USA)
Previous brand campaigns: EA Sports, Puma, VISA, La Liga

Daniel Got Hits is a pro Freestyler & viral sensation from New York City. By creating his own unique style of viral and entertaining content he’s built a massive audience of young soccer fans and has racked up tens of millions of views across Instagram and Tik Tok (he even featured in Tik Tok’s official 2020 Super Bowl commercial!)

Daniel works with lots of major brands in the U.S and worldwide, and is regarded as one of the biggest influencers in the entire sport.

Tobias Becs (Norway)
Previous brand campaigns: Footlocker, Renault, Europa League, Euro2020

Tobias Becs is the top freestyler in Norway and one of the most well-respected players in the global scene. By combining his passion for travel with his freestyle skills, he’s created his own lane and is constantly putting out new content from his trips around the world.

Lisa Freestyle (France)

Lisa Freestyle is the most followed female freestyler in the world and continues to take the world by storm with her viral street-style videos. 
Based out of Paris, she works with huge brands both in France as well as globally and is one of the biggest names in the sport of Freestyle.

Previous brand campaigns: Puma, PSG, FashionNova, FIFA

5 - Jessica Macrina (USA)

Previous brand campaigns: Lululemon, Nike, Facebook

Jessica Macrina is a LA-based soccer influencer and viral sensation.
By showcasing her athletic ability along with her lifestyle content, she’s been taking
the social media world by storm with her talent, garnering tens of millions of views across
her Instagram and Tik Tok accounts.

Before becoming a full-time creator, Jessica also played college soccer
at the highest level for Long Beach State!
