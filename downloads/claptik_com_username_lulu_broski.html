FAQ — Free TikTok Viewer & Profile Analytics

Is this TikTok viewer always free?
Yes, it’s completely free to use. There are no subscriptions, hidden fees, or paid upgrades.
Do I need to log in or create an account?
No, you don’t need to log in. The viewer works directly with public profiles and posts, so you can use it freely without an account.
Do you host, mirror, or store TikTok content?
No. We don’t host, mirror, or store any media on our servers. The tool simply retrieves and displays publicly available content from TikTok at the moment you make your request.
How do I remove a video or image from the viewer?
Since we only show publicly available data pulled directly from TikTok, any removal has to happen at the source. If a post is deleted or set to private on TikTok, it will automatically disappear from here.
Why are some posts missing or unavailable?
Posts that are private, removed, restricted by age or location, or otherwise unavailable won’t be displayed. In some cases, temporary limits from TikTok or changes to their API may also impact access.
Can I download TikTok videos, audio, or images?
You can only download media that is publicly accessible. If TikTok provides access to it, a secure download link will be generated automatically.
What analytics do you show?
From the posts currently loaded in your session, we calculate key averages such as views, likes, comments, and shares. We also identify the highest-performing post (peak views) and analyze posting frequency (posts per day). These metrics are based on real-time session data, not overall lifetime performance.
Do you track my searches?
No. We don’t log or keep any of your searches. Everything you do remains completely private.
Does it work on mobile?
Yes. The viewer is fully compatible with mobile devices, with all elements automatically adapting to fit any screen size.
