A cookie is a small file of letters and numbers that we store on your browser or the hard drive of your computer if you agree. Cookies contain information that is transferred to your computer's hard drive. 

We use the following cookies: 

Strictly necessary cookies. These are cookies that are required for the operation of our website. They include, for example, cookies that enable you to log into secure areas of our website, use a shopping cart or make use of e-billing services. 


Analytical or performance cookies. These allow us to recognise and count the number of visitors and to see how visitors move around our website when they are using it. This helps us to improve the way our website works, for example, by ensuring that users are finding what they are looking for easily.  Functionality cookies. These are used to recognise you when you return to our website. This enables us to personalise our content for you, greet you by name and remember your preferences (for example, your choice of language or region). 

Targeting cookies. These cookies record your visit to our website, the pages you have visited and the links you have followed. We will use this information to make our website and the advertising displayed on it more relevant to your interests. [We may also share this information with third parties for this purpose.]

6 Easy Ways To Find Trending Sounds On TikTok

Ready to tune into the trending sounds on TikTok and make your videos sing? We’ve put together six easy ways to find trending sounds on TikTok to help your videos get more reach and maybe even go viral!

Ready to tune into the trending sounds on TikTok and make your videos sing? We’ve put together six easy ways to find trending sounds on TikTok to help your videos get more reach and maybe even go viral!

1. TikTok Creative Center

If you’re posting on TikTok and you haven’t already bookmarked TikTok Creative Center then this is your note to do it. It’s effectively a guide to the platform's biggest trends, from hashtags to creators, songs and trending videos. The creative center allows you to search trending content within a set time frame and specific regions so you know what is happening across the globe, making it a great place to find not only trending sounds but trending content and hashtags.

2. TikTok’s Search Bar

You may have seen that a lot of the younger generation, Gen Z’s especially, are using TikTok as a search engine. In fact studies show that TikTok is currently deemed the fourth most-popular search engine. There is already proof that the search function in TikTok is pretty good so it’s a good place to start when looking for trending sounds in the app. Just type ‘trending sound’ in the search, tap the ‘Sounds’ tab and scroll through the list. You’ll see some sounds are labelled ‘popular’ these are the sounds that you want to be paying close attention to.

3. Check Out TikTok's Viral Playlist

Another great place to look for trending sounds on TikTok is by looking through their playlists. When you create a TikTok and select “add sound” you can use the search function to bring up the sound library, then head to ‘Discover more sounds’. This takes you to the main library area where you will find sounds recommended for you and a range of categories created by TikTok. Looking through this library, you will find popular songs to use in your next post.

4. Follow Accounts Who Post Trending Sounds On TikTok

Many creators on the platform cover trending sounds in their content. If you search for trending sounds in the app many of them will appear, we’ve linked a couple of accounts that focus their content on trending sounds.
slide 1 to 2 of 2

5. Check Out Websites Devoted To Tracking Trending TikTok Sounds

Last but not least is using websites that are devoted to tracking trending TikTok sounds. Sites like TokChart and Tokboard update their sites fairly regularly with trending sounds you can use, giving you information about the audio and how many views the sound has gained.

6. Stay Active On The Platform

One of the most fool-proof ways to identify trending TikTok sounds is to stay active on the platform. The more you engage with TikTok, the more tailored the algorithm becomes to your interests. Although the content pushed to you will align with your niche and what you’ve recently been watching, you'll soon recognise songs and sounds that keep popping up on your For You Page (FYP) again and again—these will be trending ones within your niche.


And there you have six easy ways to find trending sounds on TikTok to help boost your reach on the platform. If you’re new to TikTok or need help creating content, reach out to our social media team who will be more than happy to help or head over to our guide to learn more about how the platform can help your business.
